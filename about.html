About Us

Fiman trading is a wholly Namibian owned trading group operating in Namibia with office and workshop in Windhoek.
In over the years, the company has undertaken many challenging projects, accumulated skills and experience in handling projects.

We have been involved in delivery, Printing, Signs, Design & Advertising to both government and non-governmental organizations.
Our success is inextricably linked to our professional and technical capabilities and to our team of highly committed, skilled and trained staff.

In addition, we have strong support of our supplier and manufacturers from the Republic of South Africa (RSA) who give us access to their wealth of knowledge and products.

What We Do

Fiman Trading provides general contracting, construction management and pre-engineered metal building services to a broad range of commercial and industrial clients throughout Namibia.

We do general dealing with clients and supply their required goods within the agreed delivery time.
