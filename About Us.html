About Us

Fiman trading is a wholly Namibian owned trading group operating in Namibia with office and workshop in Windhoek. In over the years, the company has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills, know-how experience in handling projects. We have been involved in Supplies & delivery, Printing & Signs and Design & Advertising, to both government and non-governmental organizations. Our success is inextricably linked to our professional and technical capabilities and to our team of highly committed, skilled and trained staff. In addition, we have strong support of our supplier and manufacturers from the Republic of South Africa (RSA) who give us access to their wealth of knowledge and products.
